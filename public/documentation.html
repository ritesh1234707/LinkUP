Getting Started

What is LinkUP?

LinkUP is a platform that connects event organizers with potential sponsors. Our goal is to simplify the sponsorship process and create meaningful partnerships between events and brands.

Account Creation

To use LinkUP, you need to create an account as either an event organizer or a sponsor:

  1. Click on "Sign Up" from the homepage or login page
  2. Fill in your details including name, email, and password
  3. Select your role: "Event Organizer" or "Sponsor"
  4. Click "Create Account" to complete registration

Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you'll be directed to your personalized dashboard. The dashboard provides quick access to all platform features, with a different interface based on your role (organizer or sponsor).

For Event Organizers

Creating an Event

To create a new event:

  1. Click on "Add New Event" from your dashboard
  2. Fill in event details including name, date, location, and expected audience
  3. Upload event images and add a compelling description
  4. Specify your sponsorship needs and available opportunities
  5. Click "Create Event" to publish

Finding Sponsors

To browse potential sponsors:

  1. Click on "Find Sponsors" from your dashboard
  2. Use filters to narrow down sponsors by industry, budget, or location
  3. Click on a sponsor to view their full profile
  4. Use the "Contact" button to initiate a conversation

Creating Sponsorship Proposals

To create a sponsorship proposal:

  1. Navigate to "Manage Proposals" from your dashboard
  2. Click "New Proposal" and select the relevant event
  3. Choose the sponsor you want to send the proposal to
  4. Define sponsorship tiers and benefits
  5. Review and send your proposal

For Sponsors

Setting Up Your Sponsorship Profile

To set up your sponsorship profile:

  1. Click on "Edit Profile" from your dashboard
  2. Add your company details, logo, and description
  3. Specify your target audience and preferred event types
  4. Define your budget range and sponsorship preferences
  5. Save your profile to make it visible to event organizers

Browsing Events

To find events to sponsor:

  1. Click on "Find Events" from your dashboard
  2. Use filters to narrow down events by type, location, or date
  3. Click on an event to view complete details
  4. Express interest using the "Contact Organizer" button

Managing Proposals

To manage proposals from event organizers:

  1. Navigate to "View Proposals" from your dashboard
  2. Review incoming sponsorship proposals
  3. Click on a proposal to view all details and benefits
  4. Accept, decline, or negotiate terms with the organizer
  5. Track all your sponsorship commitments in one place

Account Management

Updating Your Profile

You can update your profile information at any time by clicking on your name in the top-right corner and selecting "Your Profile".

Changing Your Password

To change your password:

  1. Click on your name in the top-right corner
  2. Select "Settings"
  3. Click on "Change Password"
  4. Enter your current password and your new password
  5. Click "Update Password" to save changes

Forgot Password

If you forget your password:

  1. Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for a password reset link
  4. Click the link and create a new password

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to use LinkUP?

LinkUP offers a freemium model. Basic features are available for free, while premium features require a subscription. Check our pricing page for current rates.

Can I change my role from organizer to sponsor or vice versa?

Currently, you cannot change roles within the same account. You would need to create a new account with the desired role.

How do payments work between organizers and sponsors?

LinkUP does not handle payments directly. We facilitate the connection between organizers and sponsors, but payment arrangements are made directly between the parties.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All sensitive information is encrypted and we never share your personal data with third parties without consent.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to Settings, scroll to the bottom, and click "Delete Account". Note that this action is irreversible.
